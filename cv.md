## Research Interests & Key Findings
As a veterinarian and virologist, I study **viral immune evasion** and **zoonotic adaptation**. Combining molecular virology, antibody analysis, and fieldwork, I aim to inform vaccines/therapeutics and pandemic preparedness. [1](https://univtokyo-my.sharepoint.com/personal/5979693985_utac_u-tokyo_ac_jp/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B03F59482-2896-49E4-88DB-F3B0C9621407%7D&file=260310_CV_SF_DS_SF_SF_DS_SF%20for%20HP.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true)

### 1) Virus antigenicity & neutralizing antibodies
- Identified a **broadly neutralizing epitope** in feline calicivirus outside the hypervariable region, explaining cross‑strain activity (*Virus Research*, 2022).  
- Showed a spike substitution confers **cilgavimab resistance** via steric hindrance at the antibody–spike interface (*Viruses*, 2022).  
- Contributed to variant virology and vaccine effectiveness (e.g., *J. Infect. Dis.*, 2023; *Cell*, 2022).  
- *Nature Communications* paper (XBB) cited **~373** times（Word記載の値）。[1](https://univtokyo-my.sharepoint.com/personal/5979693985_utac_u-tokyo_ac_jp/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B03F59482-2896-49E4-88DB-F3B0C9621407%7D&file=260310_CV_SF_DS_SF_SF_DS_SF%20for%20HP.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true)

### 2) Coronavirus spillover & host range
- Mapped **ACE2 residues** (31/35) and spike 493 as determinants for bat ACE2 tropism (*J. Virol.*, 2023).  
- Demonstrated bat‑derived coronaviruses replicate poorly in **human respiratory tissues** (*EBioMedicine*, 2024); covered by **Nature News** and IMSUT press.  
- Led/participated in **bat fieldwork in Vietnam** (capture & on‑site surveillance). [1](https://univtokyo-my.sharepoint.com/personal/5979693985_utac_u-tokyo_ac_jp/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B03F59482-2896-49E4-88DB-F3B0C9621407%7D&file=260310_CV_SF_DS_SF_SF_DS_SF%20for%20HP.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true)
